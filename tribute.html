Ishmael the Snowman

Built Feb. 13, 2025

Named after the first son of Abraham, Ishmael

What he's made of

  • Three large snowballs for the base, mid-section, and head
  • Two twigs for the arms
  • Many small pinecones to make up the eyes, mouth, nose, and buttons

Build Process

  • Started with the base, mid-section, and head snowballs

    created by forming a small snowball and rolling it on the ground until it reached desired size

  • Added sticks to mid-section to create arms

    found at the base of a tree

  • Added small pinecones to give detail (eyes, buttons, etc.)

    found at the base of a tree (separate from the arm stick tree)
